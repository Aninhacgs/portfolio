Tatiana Escovedo, PhD

Received the BSc and MSc degrees in Computer Science from the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio), Brazil, in 2005 and 2007, respectively, and the PhD degree in Electrical Engineering from the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio) in 2015, in the field of Artificial Intelligence. Dr. Escovedo is currently Data Scientist at Petrobras, in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and assistant professor and course coordinator at PUC-Rio. She is the author of several books and papers in the area of software engineering and machine learning. Her research interests include Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, Machine Learning, Analytics, Business Intelligence, Management and Education.
